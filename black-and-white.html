The World is Black and White

Platforming game made for the 2020 LEAP-DAY Make-a-Level Game Jam. To collect all coins, you will have to find the hidden areas.

Instructions:
Move Left: D or Left Arrow Key
Move Right: D or Right Arrow Key
Jump: W, Up Arrow Key, or Space
